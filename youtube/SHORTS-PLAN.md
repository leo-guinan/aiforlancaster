# YouTube Shorts Plan — Prompt Demos from the Book
## Each short demos ONE prompt from the book in under 60 seconds

---

## FORMAT (same every time)

1. HOOK (3 seconds): State the problem in one sentence
2. SCREEN RECORDING (40-50 seconds): Show the prompt being typed, AI responding
3. RESULT (5 seconds): Show the finished output
4. CTA (2 seconds): "50 more prompts in the free book. Link in bio."

**Recording method:** Screen record your phone or laptop. Type the prompt.
Show the result. Voice over it. That's it. No face needed for shorts
(though a small face cam in the corner helps with algorithm).

**Editing:** CapCut or iMovie. Speed up the typing. Slow down to show the
result. Add captions (mandatory for shorts — most people watch muted).

**Cadence:** 2-3 shorts per week. Batch record 6-8 in one sitting.

**Total production time per short:** 10-15 minutes max.
