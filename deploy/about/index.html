About

Who

Leo Guinan. Lancaster, Ohio. I help small businesses figure out what AI actually does for them — and more importantly, what it doesn't.

This site is my public notebook. Free guides, no affiliate links, no sponsors. If something costs money, I say so. If it's hype, I say that too.

Why Lancaster

Because Main Street businesses get sold AI by people who've never run one. The tools are real, the savings are real, but nobody's explaining it in plain English without a sales pitch at the end.

I'm fixing that, one guide at a time.
