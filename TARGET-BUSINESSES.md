## STRATEGY

Don't email 100 businesses. Email 10 with something specific and useful.

The approach: For each business, visit their website and social media.
Identify ONE specific thing AI could improve for them. Send an email that
demonstrates you already understand their business. Not a pitch — a gift.

"Hey, I noticed [specific thing]. Here's how I'd fix it with AI. I already
built a quick demo. Want to see it?"

## THE "GIFT" APPROACH (Better than cold email)

Instead of just emailing, create something FOR them first:

1. **For a restaurant**: Use AI to rewrite their Google Business Profile
   description. Send them the before/after. "I did this for free. If you
   want, I'll update it for you."

2. **For an HVAC company**: Record yourself calling their business line.
   Time how long it takes to get through. Show them the data. "You missed
   2 of my 3 calls. Here's what an AI receptionist sounds like." [Play demo]

3. **For an attorney**: Take their website's About page. Rewrite it with
   AI. Send both versions. "Which one would you trust more as a potential
   client?"

4. **For any business**: Check their Google reviews. Use AI to draft
   responses to their unanswered reviews. Send them. "These are ready to
   post if you want them."

This approach costs you nothing but time, and it demonstrates capability
instead of claiming it.
