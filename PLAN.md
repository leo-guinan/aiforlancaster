## THE STRATEGY (In order)

### Track 1: The Book (Weeks 1-3)
The book is the authority artifact. It exists to be handed to people.
Whether anyone buys it on Amazon is secondary to the fact that it EXISTS.

1. Write the book using AI-assisted drafting (Leo edits for voice)
2. Publish on Amazon KDP (Kindle + paperback)
3. Create landing page at aiforlancaster.com
   - Email capture → free PDF download
   - Amazon listing exists for credibility
   - Physical copies ordered at cost (~$3-4 each, order 50 = ~$200)
4. Each physical copy has Leo's contact info in Chapter 12
5. Hand them out like business cards, but better

### Track 2: First Client (Weeks 1-4, parallel with book)
Don't wait for the book to be done. Start outreach NOW.

1. Research 20 target businesses from chamber directory
2. For top 5: build a specific "gift" — something free that demonstrates value
   - Rewritten Google listing
   - Unanswered review responses
   - Demo voice agent call recording
   - Rewritten About page
3. Send personalized outreach (not mass email)
4. Take meetings, do free assessments
5. Close first client → use revenue to join chamber

### Track 3: Chamber Membership (After first client)
$500 annual fee. Pay it from first client revenue.

1. Join as MetaSPN
2. Attend Chamber Connect (monthly networking)
3. Attend YPFC events (young professionals — early adopters)
4. After 2-3 events: propose free lunch-and-learn
5. Use the book as the follow-up resource

## THE PRODUCT MENU

### Tier 1: The Book ($0-$15)
- Free PDF via email signup
- $9.99 Kindle / $14.99 paperback on Amazon
- Pure lead generation and credibility

### Tier 2: Free AI Assessment ($0)
- 30-minute consultation
- Walk through their business, identify top 3 AI opportunities
- Write up a one-page summary
- 15-30% convert to Tier 3

### Tier 3: AI Implementation ($2,500 setup + $250/month)
- Voice agents for phone handling
- Basic automations (email, scheduling, CRM)
- Custom setup for their specific business
- 30 days of monitoring included
- Ongoing monthly support + maintenance

### Tier 4: Custom Development ($5,000-$15,000)
- Custom apps built for their workflow
- CRM replacement (the Replit model from the podcast)
- Complex multi-tool automations
- Only offer this after you have 5+ Tier 3 clients

## THE MATH

### To Break Even on Chamber Membership:
- 1 client at Tier 3 = $2,500 setup fee
- Chamber costs $500/year
- Net after chamber: $2,000 + $250/month recurring

### Monthly Target (6 months out):
- 10 Tier 3 clients × $250/month = $2,500/month recurring
- 1-2 new clients/month × $2,500 setup = $2,500-$5,000
- Total: $5,000-$7,500/month
- Costs: ~$1,500/month (platforms, tools, chamber, gas)
- Net: $3,500-$6,000/month

### Annual Target (12 months out):
- 20 Tier 3 clients × $250/month = $5,000/month recurring
- Book sales (modest): $200-$500/month
- Occasional Tier 4 project: $5,000-$15,000 quarterly
- Total: ~$80,000-$100,000/year from local AI consulting alone
