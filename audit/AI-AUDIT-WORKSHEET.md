## Section 3: The Opportunity Map

Take every task from Section 2 where you marked at least one Y. Write it below. Then score it.

**How easy to fix with AI?**
- **Easy:** You can do it with a free tool, today, with copy-paste or simple typing. No integrations, no custom setup.
- **Medium:** You need to connect something to something else, customize a template, or spend a few hours configuring. Doable in a weekend.
- **Hard:** You need someone to build something, change a business process, or do real technical setup. "Hire someone" territory.

**How much time would it save?**
- **Low:** Less than 1 hour per week
- **Medium:** 1-3 hours per week
- **High:** 3+ hours per week

### Priority Scoring Guide

| | **High Time Savings** | **Medium Time Savings** | **Low Time Savings** |
|---|---|---|---|

The Monday Morning Test: Could you implement this before lunch on Monday? If yes, it's a DO FIRST. If you'd need to research tools, set up integrations, or hire someone — it's PLAN FOR LATER.
